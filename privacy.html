Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

OmniPad AI by Tenora Labs respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

Account Data: Email and password via Firebase Authentication when you sign up.

Chat Data: Your messages are sent to Google Gemini AI to generate responses. We do not store chat content permanently.

Usage Data: Credit balance, login timestamps via Supabase. Used to manage free credits and prevent abuse.

2. Third Party Services

OmniPad AI uses:

  • Firebase: Authentication and user accounts. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI: Processes your prompts to generate AI responses.
  • Supabase: Stores credit balance and usage logs.
  • Google AdSense/AdMob: Displays ads if you choose to watch them for credits. Subject to Google Privacy Policy
  • Paystack: Processes Premium subscription payments if you upgrade.

3. Cookies & Ads

We use cookies for authentication and Google AdSense may use cookies for personalized ads. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Passwords are hashed by Firebase and never stored by us.
